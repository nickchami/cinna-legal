1. About Cinna

Cinna is a platform that connects riders with drivers and bus operators for intercity transportation. Cinna is not a transportation company and does not own, operate, or control vehicles. We facilitate cost‑sharing carpools and bus bookings between independent users.

2. Changes to the Terms

Cinna may modify the Service or these Terms at any time. Updates will be posted with the effective date. Continued use after changes = acceptance.

3. Eligibility & Accounts

  • You must be at least in the year you are turning 18 to create an account.
  • Provide accurate, up‑to‑date information.
  • Keep credentials confidential; you’re responsible for account activity.

4. Vehicle & Driver Requirements

  • Valid, full, non‑expired driver’s licence (no learner/restricted).
  • Maintain valid vehicle insurance.
  • Vehicle profile: type, capacity, seasonal equipment (e.g., winter tires Nov–Mar), luggage space, photos.
  • False/expired documentation may result in suspension.

5. Nature of the Service

  • Carpools: cost‑sharing only (no profit beyond costs).
  • Bus Services: provided by independent third‑party operators.

6. Your Responsibilities

  • Register via school email and keep info current.
  • Use common sense and caution in interactions.
  • Member disputes are resolved between users except where law requires otherwise.

7. Conduct Guidelines

  • Follow laws and drive safely/soberly.
  • Respect IP and privacy; don’t misuse the Service.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or abuse.
  • No commercial use; trips must be cost‑sharing only.
  • No speculative trips; post only actual planned trips.

8. Using the Service

  • Trips must be arranged via the Service; no unofficial stops/services.
  • Posted Driver must be the actual driver.
  • All payments go through Cinna; no cash.

9. Cancellation, No‑Shows & Refunds

Rider Cancellation (Carpool)

  • ≥72h before departure: full refund.
  • 72h–24h: refund decreases on a curve.
  • <24h: no refund.
  • Mistake policy: cancel within 10 minutes of purchase → full refund.

Rider Cancellation (Bus)

  • Same curve (100% ≥72h → 0% ≤24h), less fees.

Driver Cancellation (Strike Policy)

  • Cancel <10h before departure → 1 strike.
  • 3 strikes in a rolling year → suspension review.
  • Riders receive 100% refund inc. service fees.

Driver On‑Time Policy

  • Depart >15 min late → 10% payout deduction for that trip.

No‑Shows

  • Passenger: misses 15‑minute pickup window → no refund (unless driver agrees).
  • Driver: full refund to passengers + no‑show review.

10. Payout Policies

  • Bus Vendors: paid every second Friday for tickets past the 24h refund window.
  • Carpool Drivers: paid weekly (Fridays) for trips past a 48h post‑trip buffer.

11. Carpool Price Cap Policy

  • One‑average model: flat 18¢/km cap across vehicles; driver may adjust downward.

12. Ratings & Reviews

  • Maintain ≥4.5/5 average; 10‑trip grace for new drivers.
  • Low ratings → 2‑week Quality Review; continued issues may suspend.
  • Safety incidents → immediate review.

13. Anti‑Discrimination & Harassment

  • No discrimination on protected characteristics.
  • No harassment (verbal, physical, digital) or sexual misconduct.
  • Report violations promptly.

14. Identity Verification

  • ID verification (photo ID, selfie) may be required before posting trips.
  • Refusal/failure may block access. Background checks are not guaranteed.

15. Privacy Policy

We follow PIPEDA. See our Privacy Policy for data collection/use/sharing details.

16. Liability & Disclaimers

  • Cinna isn’t responsible for accidents, delays, cancellations, or losses.
  • Drivers/Riders are responsible for insurance and legal compliance.
  • Service provided “as‑is,” without warranties.

17. Termination

We may suspend or terminate accounts for Terms violations, fraud, or harmful conduct.

18. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

  • Governing law: Canada.
  • Resolve disputes between Members first; then escalate to Cinna Support.
